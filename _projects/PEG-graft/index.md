title: PEGylated Thrombomodulin Coatings for Decellularized Vascular Grafts
description:  A biomaterials strategy to reduce thrombosis and improve endothelialization in decellularized vascular grafts. Designed a surface modification approach using PEGylated thrombomodulin to functionalize decellularized vascular grafts. The bidirectional coating was intended to improve hemocompatibility by reducing clot formation while promoting endothelial cell attachment. Analyzed the trade-offs between bioactivity, stability, and mechanical integrity, framing the design within clinical constraints of implantable vascular devices.
